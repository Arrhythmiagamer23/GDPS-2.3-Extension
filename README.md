### 2.3 Features for GDPS - A fork of Remina GDPS mod but with more features to use for your GDPS
This GDPS is full of 2.3 features by fans and they are ported to this GDPS coming soon!
You can create anything in part of game engineering.

This mod from Remina GDPS was modified and simplified to match the looks of the Geometry Dash Game.

This mod also requires the Forked .geode mod of Game Objects factory that supports pads, dash orbs, speed portals, gamemode portals and more.<br>
https://github.com/Arrhythmiagamer23/geode-game-objects-factory/releases

## Why is it not in the geode index?
They said this to Lil2kki.
```ps
This mod shouldn't be used on the main game's server,
as it could lead to tons of problems for users 
without the mod and also potentially the server. 
It's a good idea though, I will say.
```
You can experiment and try it to make something cool, but don't upload any levels unless you have your own GDPS you made to add and be able to create with it only in the GDPS you own.

Feel free to use it on your own GDPS.
